Air Analytics

Objective


At Air Analytics we set out to find out the true story behind airlines and their prices. For this project, we selected Nov 15th, 2019 as our target date to analyze, as it is a long weekend for Mexican workers. It is more likely that the demand for flights for this weekend in particular will be high. We look forward to help you answer these questions:

  • Will the price increase as the departure day gets closer?

  • Is there a day of the week when prices are lower?

  • Why not just use websites such as Despegar.com, or cellphone apps like Hopper?

It's a well known fact that airlines change prices constantly. They even use cookies to tell if you have been looking for a specific flight and adjust prices accordingly. As Data Scientists we shall uncover the truth.
